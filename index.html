User manual

What every tool does, in one glance.

Edit PDFRewrite existing text, add formatted text boxes, draw, highlight, erase, whiteout, add movable shapes, stamps and images — then export.
Edit existing textChoose the text-cursor tool and click any line to rewrite it in place. Works on digital PDFs (not scans).
WhiteoutDrag a box over anything to hide it — covered with the page's own background colour.
Dark & light modeUse the moon/sun button in the header. Your choice is remembered on this device.
Organize pagesReorder by dragging, rotate, duplicate or delete. Insert blank pages, other PDFs or images.
MergeCombine every open PDF into one file. Drag to set the order right inside the tool.
SplitExtract a page range, or burst the document into one PDF per page.
WatermarkStamp text or a logo across every page, with adjustable opacity and size.
Page numbersAdd numbering in the position and format you choose.
CompressTwo modes: keep text sharp & selectable, or maximum shrink. Always shows before/after size.
Rotate allTurn every page 90°, 180° or 270° in one click.
Export anywhereEvery result can be saved as PDF, Word, PowerPoint, Excel or images.
